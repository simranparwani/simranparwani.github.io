About the film

This film was produced for our first day of Communications Lab class. I remember thinking that the professor was joking when he said we were going to shoot a short film for 30 minutes and then edit for 30 minutes...on our first day of class. I loved being thrown into the class and working with my classmates so early in the course. The film is part of the NYUAD 30 Minute Film Festival. -Simran Parwani, Production Assistant
